The closest rapid transit to MIT is the Kendall/MIT MBTA station. There are several options to travel from the Logan Airport to the MIT area.

Rideshare App (Recommended in general, ~20 minutes, $20-30 depending on the exact destination)

  • Follow the "Ride App" sign at the airport and go to the rideshare pickup area in the central parking garage. There is one area for Terminals A and E and another for Terminals B and C.

MBTA Silver Line + Red Line (Recommended if your accomodation is close to an MBTA station, ~45 minutes, $2.40):

  • Take the free Silver Line SL1 bus from any Logan terminal (arrival floor)
  • Get off at South Station
  • Transfer to the Red Line towards Alewife
  • Get off at Kendall/MIT station
