Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the HSP 2026 Conference

Registration & Payment

How do I register for the conference? +
Registration will open in February 2026. You can register online through our website or contact our registration team directly. Early bird registration is available until February 28, 2026.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For institutional payments, we can provide invoices upon request.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +
Full refunds are available until April 30, 2026. After that date, a 50% refund is available until May 31, 2026. No refunds will be issued after May 31, 2026.

Abstract Submission

What is the deadline for abstract submission? +
The deadline for abstract submission is January 15, 2026. Late submissions will not be considered.
What format should my abstract be in? +
Abstracts should be submitted in PDF format, maximum 300 words, using the template provided on our website. Include title, authors, affiliations, and a brief description of your research.
When will I be notified about my abstract acceptance? +
Notification of acceptance will be sent by March 15, 2026. You will be informed about the format of your presentation (oral, poster, or workshop).

Conference Details

Where is the conference being held? +
The conference will be held at the MIT Campus in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Specific venue details and directions will be provided closer to the conference date.
Is accommodation included in the registration fee? +
Accommodation is not included in the registration fee. However, we have secured discounted rates at nearby hotels. Information about accommodation options will be provided after registration.
Will meals be provided during the conference? +
Yes, lunch and coffee breaks are included in the registration fee. Breakfast and dinner are not included, but there are many dining options available on campus and in the surrounding area.
Is there a dress code for the conference? +
Business casual attire is recommended. The conference is professional but not formal, so comfortable clothing is appropriate.

Technical Support

What equipment will be available for presentations? +
Each presentation room will be equipped with a computer, projector, microphone, and laser pointer. Presenters should bring their presentations on a USB drive or upload them in advance.
Will there be Wi-Fi available? +
Yes, free Wi-Fi will be available throughout the conference venue. Login credentials will be provided at registration.
Can I record presentations? +
Recording of presentations is not permitted without explicit permission from the presenter. Photography for personal use is allowed, but please be respectful of presenters and other attendees.
